Woodstower: Lyon's eco-friendly music festival.
29/08/2024
Since 1998, Woodstower has been committed to creating a fun and responsible music festival that respects the environment. It's now one of France's top 5 eco-festivals! The festival takes sustainability seriously, from its logistics and communication to its food choices and partnerships. Held in the Grand Parc Miribel Jonage, a Natura 2000 protected area, Woodstower is dedicated to preserving this beautiful environment.
