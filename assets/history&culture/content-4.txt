Exploring Lyon's Heritage

The Old Town (Vieux Lyon), a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is a maze of cobblestone streets, medieval buildings, and hidden courtyards. Fourvière Hill offers panoramic views of the city and is home to the Basilica of Notre-Dame de Fourvière, a prominent landmark. Explore the city's history and culture at the Musée Gadagne, the Musée des Beaux-Arts, and the Musée des Tissus et des Arts Décoratifs.
